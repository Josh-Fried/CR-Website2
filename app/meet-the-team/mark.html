Mark Tronson

Co-Owner

Mark Tronson Co-Owner Mark brings a strong entrepreneurial spirit and a lifelong passion for business. He launched his first venture—a print and copy shop—at age 21 and has been independently self-employed ever since. Over a 27-year career in the printing industry, he earned numerous industry awards before successfully selling the business.

Today, Mark is the owner and co-owner of several ventures, including restaurants, retail wine shops, a coffee establishment, and most recently, a wine bar.

For the Costa Rica project, Mark contributes his extensive marketing background and a keen focus on the details that create exceptional guest experiences. He is deeply invested in ensuring visitors enjoy a memorable and seamless stay in this tropical paradise.
