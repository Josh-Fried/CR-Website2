Mark Tronson

Co-Owner

Mark Tronson brings a strong entrepreneurial spirit and a lifelong passion for business to Triada Collections. He launched his first venture - a print and copy shop - at the age of 21 and has remained independently self-employed ever since. Over a 27-year career in the printing industry, Mark earned numerous industry awards before successfully selling the business.

Today, Mark owns and co-owns several ventures across the hospitality and retail sectors, including restaurants, wine shops, a coffee establishment, and most recently, a wine bar. His broad business experience reflects both his versatility as an operator and his instinct for building brands and customer-centered experiences.

For the Costa Rica project, Mark contributes his extensive marketing background and a keen focus on the details that shape exceptional guest experiences. He remains deeply invested in helping create a stay that is both memorable and seamless for owners and visitors alike.
