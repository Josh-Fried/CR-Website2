Allen Wishman

Development Consultant

Allen Wishman is a seasoned data and AI strategist, enterprise technology executive, angel investor, and entrepreneur who helps organizations drive innovation through advanced technology and operational strategy.

With more than 20 years of experience leading data, technology, and product organizations, Allen brings a proven ability to shape strategic vision and deliver high-impact outcomes. His leadership has supported large-scale transformation across cybersecurity, data ecosystems, and AI implementation for global Fortune 100 and post-IPO enterprises.

In the luxury real estate space, Allen advises developers on technology strategy and implementation, helping create intuitive, modern experiences that enhance both operations and owner satisfaction. His perspective brings a forward-looking approach to how technology can support thoughtful development and elevate the overall residential experience.

Allen is also a dedicated community volunteer and advocate for technology education. He remains committed to helping organizations unlock potential, accelerate growth, and build with innovation in mind.
