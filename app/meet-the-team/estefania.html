Estefanía Arias

COO

Estefanía Arias V. is a proud Costa Rican, born and raised, whose values, heritage, and deep local knowledge are foundational to the vision and execution of this exceptional project.

She currently serves as the on-site Chief Operating Officer for the development team, providing strategic leadership, hands-on supervision, and clear direction throughout the construction of luxury residences in the Papagayo Bay area. In this role, Estefanía bridges vision and execution, helping ensure that each home meets the highest standards of quality, design, and long-term value.

Having lived in Guanacaste for more than 20 years, Estefanía has witnessed firsthand the region’s transformation, growth, and extraordinary potential. That long-standing connection to the area allows her to approach development with a deep understanding of the land, the community, and the expectations of both local and international owners.

Real estate has been part of her life from an early age, having grown up immersed in the industry through her father’s career as a broker. Building on that foundation, she has spent the past decade working in high-end hospitality and residential operations, holding leadership roles across property management, concierge services, and executive operations. Her experience includes independently providing comprehensive property management and administrative services for the Luxus Team and their homes in Peninsula Papagayo, serving as Director of Operations for PEXS in the Peninsula Papagayo area, and acting as Director of Trust in Las Catalinas.

These combined experiences across development, hospitality, and operational leadership have equipped Estefanía with a comprehensive perspective and the expertise required to lead, collaborate, and deliver excellence. She is honored to guide a talented multidisciplinary team in bringing the vision of Triada Collections to life with integrity, precision, and a commitment to exceptional living.
