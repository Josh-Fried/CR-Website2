Estefanía Arias

COO

Estefanía Arias V. is a proud Costa Rican, born and raised, deeply inspired to bring my values, heritage, and local essence to this exceptional project.

I currently serve as the on-site COO for the development team, where I provide strategic leadership, hands-on supervision, and clear direction throughout the construction of luxury residences in the Papagayo Bay area. My role bridges vision and execution, ensuring that each home meets the highest standards of quality, design, and long-term value.

Having lived in Guanacaste for over 20 years, I have witnessed firsthand the region’s transformation, growth, and extraordinary potential. This long-standing connection to the area allows me to approach development with a deep understanding of the land, the community, and the expectations of both local and international owners.

Real estate has been part of my life from an early age, as I grew up immersed in the industry through my father’s career as a broker. Building on that foundation, I have spent the past decade working in high-end hospitality and residential operations, holding leadership roles across property management, concierge services, and executive operations. My experience includes independently providing comprehensive property management and administrative services for the Luxus Team on their homes in Peninsula Papagayo, serving as Director of Operations for PEXS in the Peninsula Papagayo area, and acting as Director of Trust in Las Catalinas.

These combined experiences across development, hospitality, and operational leadership have equipped me with a comprehensive perspective and the expertise required to lead, collaborate, and deliver excellence. I am honored to guide a talented multidisciplinary team in bringing the vision of Triada Collections to life with integrity, precision, and a commitment to exceptional living.
