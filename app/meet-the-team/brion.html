Brion Lindseth

CEO & Co-Owner

Brion Lindseth Chief Executive Officer / Co-Owner Brion Lindseth, CPA, JD, is a Montana attorney and CPA whose career integrates financial expertise, legal strategy, and hands-on real estate development. He began his professional journey as a Certified Public Accountant with KPMG, Peat Marwick, working in financial auditing and tax before attending law school. After practicing with a regional firm, Brion founded KLB Business Law, P.C., where he structured and closed hundreds of transactions involving complex real estate ventures, business acquisitions, high-value ranch and water-rights matters, and sophisticated commercial agreements.

For more than two decades, Brion has also served as a principal, manager, and strategic advisor to numerous real estate development and operating companies. His work includes substantial involvement in several of Montana’s most notable contemporary developments, each reflecting his ability to blend legal precision, financial discipline, and practical development insight.

Since 2017, Brion has been a national advisor on Opportunity Zones, helping municipalities, investors, and developers implement qualified projects across the country.
