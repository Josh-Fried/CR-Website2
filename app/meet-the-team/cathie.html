Cathie Williamson

CFO & Co-Owner

Cathie is the current president of Eagle Beverage, a premier Montana beer and wine distribution company. She grew up in a small northern Montana town, where she learned early the value of hard work and responsibility through her family business. A standout student, Cathie graduated as her high school’s Valedictorian before attending the University of Arizona, where she earned a degree in chemistry.

Cathie also shares a lifelong love of golf with her father. She played on the University of Arizona Wildcats golf team and, after graduating, pursued professional golf. After achieving notable success, she chose to return to the family business, where she has been an integral part of its growth and success since 2009.

For our Costa Rica project, Cathie serves as the CFO, overseeing bill payments, serving on the Architectural and Design Committee, and sitting on the HOA Board of Directors. She is also actively involved in marketing and brings a strong visionary perspective to the project. Cathie is known for being smart, dedicated, and deeply committed.  The thing she loves most about Costa Rica is how quietly it teaches her to slow down, be present, and appreciate everything in front of her.
