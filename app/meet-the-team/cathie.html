Cathie Williamson

CFO & Co-Owner

Cathie Williamson brings a unique combination of operational leadership, financial oversight, competitive discipline, and entrepreneurial perspective to the Costa Rica project.

She currently serves as President of Eagle Beverage, a premier Montana beer and wine distribution company, where she plays a central role in guiding the business’s continued growth and long-term success. Raised in a small town in northern Montana, Cathie learned early the value of hard work, responsibility, and stewardship through her family’s business. A standout student, she graduated as her high school valedictorian before attending the University of Arizona, where she earned a degree in chemistry.

Cathie also shares a lifelong love of golf with her father. She competed as a member of the University of Arizona Wildcats golf team and, following graduation, pursued professional golf. After achieving notable success in the sport, she returned to the family business, where she has been an integral part of its leadership and evolution since 2009.

For the Costa Rica project, Cathie serves as Chief Financial Officer, overseeing financial administration and bill management while also contributing to broader project leadership. She serves on the Architectural and Design Committee, sits on the HOA Board of Directors, and is actively involved in marketing and strategic vision for the development. Cathie brings thoughtful judgment, discipline, and a strong long-range perspective to every aspect of the project.

Costa Rica has become especially meaningful to Cathie for the way it invites a slower pace, deeper presence, and a greater appreciation for the beauty and simplicity of everyday life.
