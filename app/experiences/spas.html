Designed as the centerpiece of well-being within Papagayo Bay, the Spa Sanctuaries are envisioned as havens of relaxation and renewal inspired by Guanacaste’s tranquil landscape. Drawing on the language of water, stone, and tropical greenery, residents can be enveloped into spaces where luxury feels effortless and restoration comes naturally. Private garden cabanas, open-air hydrotherapy pools, and secluded relaxation lounges will complement the menu of services, creating a seamless balance between indulgence and inner calm. From natural textures to bespoke aromatics, every element is being thoughtfully conceived so the Spa Sanctuaries reflect individual preferences and evolving wellness journeys.

Exclusively reserved for homeowners, the sanctuaries will offer an elevated collection of treatments including volcanic-stone massages, organic botanical facials, and tropical fruit body rituals drawn from the land itself. Mindfulness experiences will take center stage: guided meditation, restorative yoga, breathwork, and sound therapy are being curated to harmonize body and spirit while honoring each resident’s personal rhythm.
