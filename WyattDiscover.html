Frequently Asked Questions

What is Costa Rica known for, and why are so many Americans moving there?

Costa Ricas exceptional quality of life marked by its stable democracy, world-class healthcare, natural beauty, and the Pura Vida lifestyle attracts Americans seeking an abundant, free, peaceful paradise in one of the worlds five Blue Zones.

How is Papagayo Bay different from the Papagayo Peninsula?

While sharing the same pristine coastline and proximity to luxury amenities as its prestigious neighbor, Papagayo Bay offers a rare opportunity to own property in an emerging destination where authentic Costa Rican culture and modern conveniences create an unprecedented value proposition.

How large is the Triada development?

Triada currently encompasses 75 acres of prime coastal property, offering a curated collection of luxury homes and condominiums. The development preserves 70% of natural areas and incorporates an extensive trail system.

What kind of community can I expect at Triada?

Our residents are a diverse mix of full-time and seasonal homeowners who value privacy and community. The design of our shared spaces encourages natural interaction while respecting personal space.

Is real estate a good investment in Costa Rica?

Investing in real estate in Costa Rica is an excellent financial move due to the country’s stable property market and economic growth. Papagayo Bay and the surrounding region (including the Baha Peninsula) are experiencing rapid growth with significant infrastructure development and luxury brand expansion.
