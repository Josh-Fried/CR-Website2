Why Papagayo Bay

Overlooking the calm waters of Costa Rica’s famed Papagayo Bay, our collection of exclusive homes and condos seamlessly integrate nature with luxurious indoor and outdoor living spaces.

Click map for interactive view

Location

As the nearest luxury resort to Guanacaste’s international airport, our development delivers the perfect balance of convenience and escape - just 20 short minutes from landing to lounging on the beach.

Amenities

Our exclusive residences enjoy easy access to world class diving, snorkeling, swimming, paddleboarding, boating and fishing with private amenities such as exclusive waterfall adventures, jungle tours, elegant beach club and on-site world class restaurants.

Ownership

Our elegant residences are built on fully titled, fee simple real property, differentiating us from other resorts in the area which offer homes on leased ground.

Value

Beyond Papagayo Bay’s natural beauty and convenience, the region offers exceptional investment potential. Real estate values in the Guanacaste region have consistently appreciated, driven by strong demand and preservation of Costa Rica’s natural beauty making our residences an exceptional long-term store of value. Papagayo Bay’s unique combination of luxury, serenity, sustainability, and economic growth provides discerning individuals a secure, generational asset and an elevated lifestyle rooted in wellness, nature and enduring value.
