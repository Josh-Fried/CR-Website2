Papagayo Bay

Land &
Location

Situated in Playa Panamá, Costa Rica—just minutes from the world-renowned Papagayo Bay—this land represents one of the most coveted stretches of coastline in Central America. Surrounded by lush hills, calm waters, and volcanic ridgelines, the terrain offers the perfect balance of elevation, accessibility, and natural elegance. The area is known for its tranquility, safety, and exclusivity, attracting high-end tourism and elite developments.
