Cuisine

World-Class Dining Right in the Bay

Culebra Bay is fast becoming a culinary destination in its own right, offering an exceptional array of dining experiences that rival major food capitals, but with ocean views and tropical breezes. From award-winning restaurants tucked inside luxury resorts to beachside grills and farm-to-table hidden gems, the region serves up everything from elevated Costa Rican cuisine to international fine dining. Whether you're enjoying freshly caught mahi-mahi with a glass of wine or sipping craft cocktails at sunset, every meal here is infused with flavor, flair, and a deep sense of place.

Located on the tranquil east shore of Culebra Bay, just 20 minutes from Liberia’s international airport, this region offers a rare balance of accessibility and seclusion. The bay’s calm, protected waters are ideal for yachting, paddleboarding, snorkeling, and swimming, while the surrounding hills provide panoramic ocean views and a lush tropical backdrop. The area is home to some of Central America’s most prestigious resorts, including the Four Seasons, Andaz Peninsula Papagayo, and Ritz-Carlton Reserve, reinforcing its reputation as a premier destination for luxury living. Unlike more remote coastal areas, Culebra Bay benefits from modern infrastructure, including paved roads, reliable utilities, and fiber-optic internet, making it equally suitable for vacation homes or year-round residence.

Beyond its natural beauty and convenience, the region offers exceptional investment potential. Real estate values on the Papagayo Peninsula have consistently appreciated, driven by strong demand and strict environmental regulations that limit overdevelopment and preserve long-term value. Foreign buyers enjoy full property rights and low annual property taxes, making Costa Rica one of the most investor-friendly destinations in Latin America. This area’s unique combination of privacy, sustainability, and economic growth attracts discerning individuals seeking more than just a home, they seek a secure, long-term asset and an elevated lifestyle rooted in wellness, nature, and enduring value.
