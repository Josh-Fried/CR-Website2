World-Class Dining Right in the Bay

Hacienda Blu

Nestled exclusively within our exclusive beach house, Hacienda Blu presents a culinary destination defined by sophistication and exquisite flavors. Open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, our intimate dining experience is designed to be a highlight of your seaside retreat. Begin your day with artisanal breakfast creations and f reshly brewed coffee as you greet the morning sun. For lunch, savor a menu of light yet refined dishes perfect for a midday escape. As evening descends, Hacienda Blu transforms into a luxurious gastronomic haven, offering a dinner service that showcases the finest locally sourced ingredients and culinary artistry, ensuring every meal is an unforgettable testament to great taste and refined ambiance.

Peninsula Steakhouse

Located exclusively within the clubhouse, Peninsula Steakhouse provides a dramatic setting for an unforgettable dinner, where floor-to-ceiling windows frame sweeping, panoramic views of the bay and ocean. As twilight settles over the water, the refined ambiance creates the perfect backdrop for a menu that is a proud celebration of the classic steakhouse, perfected. Each steak is meticulously selected and prepared with precision, serving as the flavorful centerpiece of a distinguished dining experience. Here, the stunning coastal vistas and an impeccable menu converge to offer the definitive steak and seafood dinner, ensuring a truly memorable evening.
